# SOP: List Tasks / Roadmap

Goal: provide a clear status snapshot on demand.

## Sources of Truth

- Active work: `.agents/sprint.md`
- Future work: `.agents/backlog.md`
- Bugs: `.agents/bugs.md`
- Completed work: `.agents/changelog.md`

## Steps

1. Read all 4 tracker files.
2. Summarize by sections:
- In progress
- Next up
- Blocked/bugs
- Recently completed
3. Highlight top 3 priorities and recommended next action.

## Output Style

- Keep concise and actionable.
- Never invent task status not present in tracker files.
